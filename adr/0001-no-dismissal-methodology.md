# ADR 0001: No-Dismissal Methodology

## Status

Accepted

## Context

PostHumanOS contains ambitious claims that can be mishandled either by premature dismissal or by unstructured speculation.

## Decision

The project will preserve ambitious ideas but require decomposition into mechanisms, assumptions, dependencies, risks, evidence, and validation paths.

## Consequences

Contributors may mark ideas as open gaps, unsafe, long-horizon, or blocked, but should not remove them merely because they are difficult.
